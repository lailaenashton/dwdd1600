Travel Packages

Idaho

The grass is not always greener on the other side! Right here in your own country is a beautiful destination just itching to be explored! This destination is full of nature hikes, scenic lookouts, wildlife, and more! With 27 state parks and Yellowstone, there is so much to do! You'll definitely be wanting to return to this rustic landscape often. It is the perfect break from the humdrum of life. Get in touch with nature in Idaho!

Estimated Cost: $500.00 (based on a 1-person, week-long trip)

Germany

Germany is rich in culture and history. Come to Germany to experience a dark academia aesthetic and meet some of the most genuine people. Germany is full of beautiful castles and ancient sites. Many people enjoy the cottages and country life here. Others love visiting the many historical museums. Whatever you are after, Germany can give it!

Estimated Cost: $1300.00 (based on a 1-person, week-long trip)

Japan

Since the western world learned of Japan, the obsession hasn't stopped, and for good reason! Japan is full of beautiful culture, language, arts, and delicious food. To get a real break from the day-to-day life you know, come to Japan! Its beautiful cities and landscapes will take your breath away and transport you to a different world.

Estimated Cost: $1000.00 (based on a 1-person, week-long trip)

Mexico

Mexico knows how to bring on the party! For a festive trip full of celebrations, come to Mexico! You will love its beautiful beaches and authentic people. And during the day when you aren't dancing, visit the many unique and historical sites that Mexico has to offer!

Estimated Cost: $800.00 (based on a 1-person, week-long trip)
