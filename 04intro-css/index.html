Welcome to the World of Ithmanu

Ura, the god of the Nothingness that Makes Up Everything, was the first to notice the dying planet, Ithmanu. Ura called cem's syblings to gather.
Down came Auptha, Goddess of the Stars and their pull.
Down came Onan, God of the Heat of the Heart.
Down came Erith, God of the Flowing Water.
Down came Ishu, God of the Rich Soil.
